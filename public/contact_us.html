About Us

There has been a recent surge in the number of cultural activities and family functions in and around Bhubaneswar. Taking this into account, Eastbrook landholdings private limited is launching Brookhaven Convention centre in Aiginia, Patrapada. The project area is spread across 1.2 acres which is divided in to two adjacent event sites, developed accordingly to match diverse customer demands and to provide alternatives to costumers to choose from. The site is ideally suited for marriage functions, birthday parties, thread ceremonies, alumni meets, school and college functions, conferences, trade shows, etc.
