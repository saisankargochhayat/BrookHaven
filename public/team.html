Meet our Team

Board of Directors

Debasish Mahapatro Director & CEO

Debasish, the founder Director, is a Management Graduate from Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta. He has a degree in Mathematics from St. Stephen's College, Delhi and is a Gurukul alumnus of the London School of Economics and Political Science. With a career spanning over 15 years, he brings with him a sectoral expertise across all the three business domains of Verve- natural resources, climate change and infrastructure development. He is involved in strategic planning, forging international partnerships, organizational development, and leading key business development initiatives at Verve.

Allan DaisDirector

He has 17 years of consulting experience in financial services in India, Jamaica and United Kingdom in retail and corporate banking and capital markets. He had been associated with PriceWaterHouse coopers in their financial advisory domain with specialization in equity markets. His interest areas are energy efficient technologies and innovations in construction technology.

Sanjaya GuptaDirector

Sanjaya is an M. Tech in Computer Science from BITS Pilani. He has founded CRAC Computers, a software and hardware Company. In the year 2000, he started a new company 4Cplus which is now venture funded by Angel Investors. He brings in key exposure for Verve to specific industrial sectors and an understanding of IT technology.
